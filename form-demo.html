About Formless

Formless is an innovative browser extension that revolutionizes how users interact with web forms. Our AI-powered technology automatically fills out forms based on your saved memories and preferences, making online form completion effortless and efficient.

We're building the future of form automation, combining intelligent field matching with a seamless user experience to eliminate repetitive data entry across the web.
